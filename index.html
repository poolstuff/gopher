In 1946, Gopher Bearing was founded on the principle of providing quality products, including a large local inventory at competitive prices with superior customer service. In 2001, we brought those same principles to Tucson. Along with our experienced staff, Gopher Bearing continues its dedication to knowing our customers and products. Our wide range of expertise includes fields such as:

  • HVAC
  • Wastewater Treatment
  • Recycling
  • Mining
  • Agriculture
  • Food and Beverage Processing and Packaging
  • Power Generation (Solar, Nuclear, Wind, Fossil Fuel)
  • Printing
  • Manufacturing/OEM
  • MRO

In addition to high quality products from the best manufacturers, we specialize in providing our customers with a myriad of value added services including:

  • Cost Savings Analysis
  • Inventory Reduction Programs
  • Application Engineering
  • Direct Relationships with Manufacturers
  • Critical Emergency Services
  • Large Local Inventory
  • Free Training Seminars
